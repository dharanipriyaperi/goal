About Us

We’re the GOAL team, a bunch of young enthusiasts, who get super excited about helping students to achieve their academic dreams. GOAL has emerged as an entity in to the educational world to meet the needs of the young aspirants.

Few years ago, some of our team members were interested to go abroad for their masters (they are currently living in Germany). At that time, we searched for agencies in India that could help them out in finding the right universities in coherence to their goals and profiles. During the course of searching and to our surprise, the average cost for one of the aspects was 3-4 times the cost of one university application and also we didn’t have many choices when we contacted the agencies in India. This led us to think that it is unfair to exploit the students for such high price. We decided to start a company that could provide the required services at more economical prices in collaboration with Indian students who pursued higher studies from USA, Germany, and Australia. This wide network helps us consider all the valuable aspects of a student’s profile which are important for admissions.

Our mission is to help the students achieve their dreams by being an integral part in their career through qualitative & economical services
