University selection & Application procedure

This is an aspect where every student needs to be careful and certain. It is very important to consider the right parameters/factors to select the universities.

After understanding your future plans and ambitions, we will shortlist 6 to 8 universities from countries of your choice & classify it based on your admit chances into three categories - Ambitious, Moderate & Safe.

We know that the application process can be cumbersome and laborious. Therefore, we will guide you through each and every step in successfully submitting your application before the deadline.

  • Session - To note your preferences and considerations
  • University shortlisting in accordance to your profile
  • Discussion - List of pros & cons for each selected university
  • Information on timeline of the application processes
  • List of required documents
  • Guidance in submitting your university application
